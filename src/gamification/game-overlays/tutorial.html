Fishing at Islands

Every 5 minutes, each island has a chance of spawning a certain rarity of fish. These islands have flashing outlines. Click on the rod button to fish!

Fish Rarity Guide

The rarity of fish is indicated by color. The outlines around fishable islands indicate the rarity of the fish there. Purchase better rods to catch higher class fish.

Shops & Safe Houses

After a hard day of fishing, you can buy/sell items at the shop or rest at one of the safehouses.
