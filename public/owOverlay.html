My Journey

Inspired by the Tokyo 2020 Olympics, I began weightlifting in 2022. I couldn't believe how a explosive strength sport could require so much delicate movement. Beginning the lift a touch off-balance triggers a butterfly effect that spirals into a complete loss of control.

I believe that software engineering requires a clear mind, and a clear mind is in part fueled by physical activity. To be mentally healthy, the mind needs to invest thinking power into an array of diverse topics. Thinking under physical stress is an underrated skill that disciplines the mind to retain clarity even in dire circumstances.
